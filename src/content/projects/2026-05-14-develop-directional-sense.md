This is a spatial skill-building app that helps you build directional sense, provide daily drills and challenges along with stats to keep you motivated

To view the live application - click here https://wayfinder-rho.vercel.app/

## The problem

In this Digital world where we are truly reliant on Apps and Internet, imagine if you are travelling to a new city or living in your own city not noticing around - the most basic skill would be to navigate the area

## What we built

- **Completely client side web application** — your data is safe and stored on your web browser
- **User Profile & Streaks** — to show you stats on your achievements, keep track of journal notes to help you achieve your goal
